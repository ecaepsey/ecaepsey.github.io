## Overview

Health Face is a daily tracking app designed to help users build and maintain a consistent face care routine through habit tracking and reminders.

## Features

- Daily habit tracking
- Streak & progress monitoring
- Personalized face care routines
- Push notifications & reminders

## Tech Stack

**Language:** Swift
**Framework:** SwiftUI
**Architecture:** MVVM-C + ViewState
**Backend:** Firebase
